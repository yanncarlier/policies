# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by the Developer ("we," "us," or "our") when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is handled.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide Directly
- **Images and Scanned Data:** When you use the scanning functionality, the App captures images of the items you scan.
- **Configurations and API Keys:** If you provide custom API keys (e.g., Google Gemini API), they are stored locally on your device to enable specific features of the App. 
- **Feedback and Support:** Any information you provide when contacting us for support or sending feedback.

### B. Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with the App (e.g., features used, session duration, crash reports) to improve our services.
- **Device Information:** We may collect non-identifying information such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve the App's core functionalities (e.g., scanning, analyzing, and organizing collections).
- To transmit images to third-party artificial intelligence services (such as Google’s Gemini API) for the sole purpose of image identification and metadata extraction.
- To analyze app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.
- To communicate with you regarding updates, security alerts, and support messages.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** By default, your scanned images, history, collections, and API keys are stored **locally on your device**. We do not maintain centralized servers storing your personal data or collections.
- **Data Transmission:** When the App uses AI services to analyze images, the image data is transmitted securely over the internet to the respective AI provider (e.g., Google). We do not control the data retention policies of these third-party services.
- **Security Measures:** While we implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data, no security system is impenetrable. We recommend securing your device with a passcode or biometric lock.

## 4. Third-Party Services and APIs

The App integrates with third-party services (e.g., Google Generative AI / Gemini) to provide functionality. 
- **Google Generative AI:** Images scanned using the App are sent to Google’s APIs for analysis. Your use of this feature is also subject to Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
- **Analytics and Crash Reporting:** We may use third-party analytics tools (e.g., Firebase) to collect anonymous usage and crash data.

## 5. Global Privacy Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Depending on your geographic location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:
- **Right to Access:** You can view the data stored locally on your device within the App.
- **Right to Deletion:** You have the right to delete your data. Because data is stored locally, you can exercise this right at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or deleting the App.
- **Right to Opt-Out:** You can opt-out of sharing anonymous usage data by adjusting the settings within the App (if applicable) or disabling internet access for the App.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions such as the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can take steps to remove it.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the "Send Feedback" functionality within the App.
