# Send Feedback

We are always looking to improve our App, and your feedback is incredibly valuable to us!

If you encounter any issues, have feature requests, or simply want to share your thoughts about your experience, please reach out to us at:

**[feedback@set-out.com](mailto:feedback@set-out.com)**

---

## Best Practices When Sending Feedback

To help us resolve your issue or understand your request as quickly as possible, please consider including the following information in your email:

### 1. Give Your Email a Clear Subject Line
- Example: *Bug: App crashes when scanning a specific item*
- Example: *Feature Request: Export collections to CSV*

### 2. Include Details (If reporting an issue)
- **What happened?** A brief description of the issue.
- **What did you expect to happen?** How should it have worked?
- **Steps to reproduce:** What were you doing right before the issue occurred?

### 3. Provide Device Information
- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15, Samsung Galaxy S23).
- The operating system version (e.g., iOS 17.4, Android 14).

### 4. Attach Screenshots or Context
If a picture tells a thousand words, a screenshot tells ten thousand! Attaching a screenshot or screen recording will greatly accelerate the troubleshooting process.

Thank you for helping us make our App better!
